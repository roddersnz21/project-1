About Us

Making neapolitan style pizza for over 20 years

Family owned pizzeria crazy about pizza. Baked in a woodfire oven, we're always bringing excellent quality, unforgettable flavours and great service. That's how we conquered our loyal customers and became one of the top pizzerias in Ireland.

Fresh Ingredients

We're offer the best Italian gastronomic excellence by usinig quality ingredients such as organically grown flours, vegetables and herbs. The integration between the ingredients and their mixing allows us to create new proposals and continuously update out menu.
